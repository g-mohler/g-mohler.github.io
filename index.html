Markovian Tweets

This program takes in text and generates a tweet using markovian text generation. Each word is picked based on the liklihood that it follows the previous word in the input text. For example, given the input "Hi Scott, say hi to Dave for me and say hi to Lisa for me!" the word generated after hi would have a 33% chance of being "Scott", and a 67% chance of being "to".


Example output using the U.S. Constituiton as input:
Meeting shall be held to Service for a Term of ten Years, in such imminent Danger as will not admit of delay.
