Markovian Tweets

This program takes in text and generates a tweet using markovian text generation. Each word is picked based on the liklihood that it follows the previous word in the input text. For example, given the text input "Hi Scott, say hi to Dave for me and say hi to Lisa for me!" the word generated after hi would have a 33% chance of being "Scott", and a 67% chance of being "to".
